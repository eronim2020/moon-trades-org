What We Do

Three layers. One mission. A measurable lunar economy.

Moon Trades operates at the intersection of terrestrial mining heritage and the emerging space resources industry — closing the foundational gaps that current ISRU roadmaps still treat as assumptions.

ISRU & Regolith Intelligence

Lunar geotechnical characterisation, core sampling methodology, and in-situ mineral assessment — addressing the measurement gap that sits underneath every lunar resource business case.

Autonomous Robotics

Soil intelligence systems and autonomous platforms designed for the lunar surface — built for post-gravity operational environments and human-machine collaboration.

Systems Architecture

End-to-end design for partners building lunar infrastructure, ISRU pilot operations, and the broader Mearth economy — bridging Earth-side engineering rigour with off-world operational reality.
